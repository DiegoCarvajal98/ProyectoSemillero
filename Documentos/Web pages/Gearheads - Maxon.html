      Gears

      Precision spur- and planetary gearheads matched to maxon motors. Gears are advantageously adapted directly to the desired motors in the delivery plant. The motor pinion is the input gearwheel for the first stage and is rigidly affixed to the motor shaft.

      Planetary Gearheads (GP)

      Gearheads featuring high torques. High power in a compact design with torques up to 120 Nm.

       

      For product details & specifications:

      Online Shop
      Spur Gearheads (GS)

      maxon spur gearheads run with low noise emissions and have high efficiency, with up to 2 Nm of torque.

       

      For product details & specifications:

      Online Shop
      GPX Planetary Gearheads

      maxon GPX gearheads are low-noise and low-backlash. Ideal for when high torques and speeds are needed.

       

      For product details & specifications:

      Online Shop
      Screw Drive

      Screw Drive with planetary gearheads for linear motion. With integrated axial bearing for high axial loads: metric spindles, ball screws and trapezoidal screws are available.
